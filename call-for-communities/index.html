Hong Kong Open Source Conference 2015

Call for Community Sessions of HKOSC 2015

New Community Sessions are added to HKOSC 2015 (2015.05.15-16 Fri-Sat).

Local open source communities and user groups can apply to community session.

  1. Standard Plan: a community will host a single session at HKOSC 2015.
  2. Extended Plan: a community propose alternative/extended planning for their community sessions/activities at or days before/after HKOSC 2015.

CFP Procedure for Community Sessions:

  1. Community must collect proposals through our CFP system.
  2. CFP submissions should be submited as general submissions or submissions to one of community sessions related to proposed topics.
  3. Hosts of community sessions will screen and pick one from submissions
  4. Unaccepted submissions will go back to HKOSC program committee for 2nd chance.

For interested communities, please complete this form on/before 2014.08.31. For enquiries, please feel freely to email to conf2015@opensource.hk.
